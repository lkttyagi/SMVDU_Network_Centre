Network Centre, SMVDU acts as the pivotal for providing the network and Internet services across the Campus with 24x7 availability. SMVD University Network (UnivNet) covers all Academic buildings, Hostels, Administrative Offices, Auditorium, VC Residence, Central Workshop, Medical Aid Centre, Guesthouse, faculty and staff residences.

Infrastructure

The network and internet services of the SMVD UnivNet are backed by CISCO 4507R layer 3 Core Switch, 35 Nos CISCO Layer 2 distribution Switches,38 Huawei Quidway Layer 2 Switches, CISCO Pix Firewalls for security, Cisco Router 2700 & 1800 series, Juniper Router (MX 80-AC Chassis with 2 MIC Slots), Central Authentication Gateway from Inventum and Open source Network Management System.

Network Centre houses 18 Dell / Sun / IBM servers (DHCP, DNS, Proxy, Domain controller(smvdu.ac.in), Virtualization Server, Web server(www.smvdu.ac.in), Intranet Server(intranet.smvdu.ac.in), Symantec Antivirus Server, Library Server(Libsys), Application servers for School of Mechanical Engineering, College of Management) running on latest operating system software like Ms Windows 2008R2 , Red Hat Linux and Ubuntu.

Internet Connectivity

Uninterrupted Internet facilities have been provided across the campus with 60 Mbps internet leased line connectivity from Reliance Communications and 1 Gbps National Knowledge Network connectivity from BSNL under NMEICT (National Mission on Education through Information & Communication Technology) programme .

With the use of NKN (National Knowledge Network), Network Centre provide various facilities like video conferencing, virtual classroom facility which enables co-sharing of information viz. lectures, presentations with other institutions. Within 2013-14, eight online workshops from IIT Bombay have been conducted at SMVDU under NMEICT with the use of virtual classroom namely AVIEW. This mode has opened a new arena of providing online teaching / orientation to the students / faculty of SMVDU by the Professors from IIT's, IISc's.

With the completion of Campus wide Networking Phase 3, all 31 Academic & Residential buildings across campus are directly connected to the Network Centre through Optical Fibre Cable. This OFC links serves as Back bone for providing the LAN Connectivity to more than 1200 LAN Points in these buildings.

IPDSLAM Connectivity to Level 1 & 2 Residence

Network Centre provides the Internet connectivity to Level 1 & 2 Residence through the use of IP DSLAM Switch (IP based Digital subscriber line access multiplexer Switch ) It allows the use of same telephone copper cable both for phone and internet. in short, it is the ISP level initiative of Network Centre to provide broad band connectivity to the residence faculty at SMVDU

Software Services Open Source / Proprietoy Initiatives

Network Centre advocates the use of open source tools and technologies in education and research like php , mysql, wordpress, joomla, drupal, open office, pfsense, freeNSD, Ubuntu, KDE, gimp and many more. However, proprietary applications servers are also installed at Network Centre as per demand of various departments"
